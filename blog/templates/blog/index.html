About Me

I am a web application developer with 10+ years of in-depth experience in full stack and full life cycle engineering from inception to depreciation. In a nutshell, I build front and back-end software that helps organizations address business challenges and meet their needs. I manage everything from REST & GraphQL APIs, backend Flask and Django applications, front-end React and Vue.js sites and even straight HTML/CSS/JS. My expertise lies within back-end software, and my go-to tech stack is going to have to be Python, FLASK, and Postgres running in a Microservice driven, AWS SAM environment with RabitMQ/AWSMQ.
